There's so much to do in Virginia Beach, and it's easy to fill your day with fun, adventure, and great memories. Whether you're visiting with family or friends, there are activities for all ages right by the ocean.

Start your morning by renting a surrey bikeâ€”a fun four-wheeled bicycle you can ride with your whole group. Pedal up and down the boardwalk, take in the views, and enjoy the ocean breeze. It's a great way to start your day with laughter and fresh air.

Things to do in the Water
  • Swim in the ocean and splash in the waves
  • Rent a cabana or beach chairs and relax on the sand
  • Rent a surfboard or boogie board and learn to ride like the pros

Fun with Kids
  • Visit the Virginia Aquarium & Marine Science Center to see sea turtles, sharks, and even touch stingrays
  • Play a round of mini putt at one of the colorful courses near the beach

Things to do on Land
  • Take pictures at the King Neptune statue, a 34-foot-tall ocean god that's perfect for your vacation photos
  • Attend a live concert at the oceanfront with music, dancing, and family fun
  • Relax by the pool at your hotel or resort and soak up the sun

Many of these activities are just steps from the beach, so it's easy to go from one to the next. You can spend the whole day exploring and never run out of things to do.

Whether you love the ocean, wildlife, sports, music, or just relaxing in the sun, Virginia Beach has something for everyone. Come make amazing memories at the coast!
